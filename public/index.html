Our Customers

  • Unformalized buying groups
  • Informal retailers
  • School nutrition programs
  • Caterers and events
  • Donor organisations
  • NGOs and NPOs

WHAT WE DO

Stockdirect virtual wholesale facilitates a relationship for its customers to purchase consumer goods in bulk as a single transaction.

HOW WE DO IT

Stokdirect receives manufacturer products at its forward stock locations, which offer multi-user storage that is flexible to demand and cost effective, and then we conveniently distribute those products to our customers directly.
