About Us

Stokdirect Virtual Wholesale is a stock forwarding e-commerce platform for Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) that services an intermediary between FMCG manufacturers and our buyers. Formed in 2022, StokDirect intends to aggregate bulk sales whilst offering real-time data to FMCG manufacturers.
