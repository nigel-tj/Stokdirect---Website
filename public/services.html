Last-mile Distribution

With our strategically located forward stock locations (FSLs) we are able to facilitate the entire process of last-mile distribution for our FMCG manufacturers products directly to our customers, which mainly includes; spaza shops, tuck shops, independent retailers, stokvel groups, feeding schemes, donor organisations and other individual groups who purchase consumer goods in bulk on our e-commerce platform.

Leveraging our independent owner driver network, we are able to carefully manage, that last mile deliveries are insured, safe, reliable and cost-effective to our customers across Africa.

Consumer behaviour and insights

Stokdirect delivers an innovative route-to-market channel for consumer goods producers in Africa and provides Real-time data from actual transactions that enable manufacturers to get their products into the right consumer market at the right time.

This enables our manufacturers and goods producers to also use this data to:

  • Cross compare sales against and improve on that.
  • See where your products rank against other manufacturers
  • Run effective promotions and campaigns directly with our customers who in drive sales traffic for your products
  • Launch new products and get samples to customers according to accurate data provided
  • Use our banner store network to launch engaging location based advertising campaigns and digital pamphlets to end-consumers

Forward stock handling and management

Our company provides forward stock locations to help FMCG manufacturers effectively last-mile distribution throughout Africa. With our strategically located FSL network, we provide FMCG manufacturers the ability to significantly reduce costs by eliminating the need to staff and operate facilities throughout the continent.
