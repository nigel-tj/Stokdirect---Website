Our Services

Our e-commerce platform mainly drives sales traffic for FMCG manufacturers through Stokdirect branded banner stores. The platform also attracts bulk sales from buying groups, stokvels, independent retailers, school nutrition programs, donor organisations, caterers, hospitality groups and other groups who want to purchase directly from manufacturers to benefit from economies of scale.

Bulk buying e-commerce platform

Our software allow our customers to:

  • View all manufacturer goods and place bulk orders, taking advantage of product promotions and campaigns
  • Track deliveries live and receive real time status and in-app message updates
  • 24/7 customer support available through in-app messaging services

Last-mile Distribution

Leveraging our independent owner driver network, we are able to carefully manage, that last mile deliveries are insured, safe, reliable and cost-effective to our customers across Africa.

Compliance with ISO standards and all related safety, health, environmental and quality regulations are strictly implemented across all our FSLs.

Consumer behaviour and insights

Stokdirect delivers an innovative route-to-market channel for consumer goods producers in and outside Africa, provides Real-time data from actual transactions that enable manufacturers to get their products into the right consumer market at the right time.

This enables our manufacturers and goods producers to also use this data to:

  • Cross compare product sales against each other and improve on that.
  • See where your products rank against other manufacturers
  • Run effective promotions and campaigns directly with our customers who in drive sales traffic for your products
  • Launch new products and get samples to customers according to accurate data provided
  • Use our banner store network to launch engaging location based advertising campaigns and digital pamphlets to end-consumers
