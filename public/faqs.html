What Is a StokDirect Buyer?
A store owner, banner store, bulk buying group, individual and group members that have registered to use our platform and get the benefits of our efficient ordering and delivery process. We mediate this process using our software, our unique logistical approach and multi warehousing manufacturer partnerships that are able to guarantee efficient product delivery.
What is a StokDirect Banner Store?
Members or buyers that have their stores branded under our logo that we are able to drive sales through them using direct to market methods that advertise specials from manufacturers.

  • Delivery timeframes

How do I cancel an order: Shop / Driver

If your order has not yet been collected, then you are able to cancel using your app.

If your order has already been collected and is enroute to your delivery, and you cancel, you are still liable for the delivery fee including the return fee to the depot.

- Get in touch with our Support Team for a refund less the initial delivery and return fee

What is a StokDirect Manufacturer?
An organization or company that produces finished goods and then sells the goods direct to our members at cost pricing or bulk discounted pricing.

I received damaged goods: Shop / Driver

Please log a call with our Support for assistance with your order number and product id of the item that is damaged.

  1. Attach an image of the damaged item.
  2. The damaged item will be collected at the next delivery.

N.B: If item is discovered to be damaged at collection point, kindly make note with Dispatch team to replace before you leave on your delivery

I got Hijacked/Robbed

Please get in contact with our Driver Support immediately with the following documents:

  • Affidavit of the incident along with the case number
  • Delivery Invoice if in possession or Delivery ID
When do i Get paid: Manufacturer / Driver

As a manufacturer you receive immediate payment of the order that has been placed.

As a driver, you will receive your payment 24-48 hours after the order has been delivered.
