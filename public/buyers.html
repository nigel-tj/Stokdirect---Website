Buyers

We know unexpected changes happen and so do our customers. With orders assigned to our driver-partners, customers receive a link to a dashboard that monitors in real time the order preparation status, driver, vehicle and route details including ETA.

Customers get an alert, either by sms, email or both, from the time the order is placed, packed and until their delivery arrives.

When our driver partner gets close to completing their stop, our customer receives a final update with a new ETA. This sets the expectation and preparation of receiving the delivery.

What Is a StokDirect Buyer?

A store owner, banner store, bulk buying group, individual and group members that have registered to use our platform and get the benefits of our efficient ordering and delivery process. We mediate this process using our software, our unique logistical approach and multi warehousing manufacturer partnerships that are able to guarantee efficient product delivery.

What is a StokDirect Banner Store?

Members or buyers that have their stores branded under our logo that we are able to drive sales through them using direct to market methods that advertise specials from manufacturers.
